The Game

You are part of a group of {{ n }} participants. The game lasts {{ num_rounds }} rounds, and the rules are the same in every round.

Each round, every participant chooses an integer between 0 and 100.

Your earnings each round

Your earnings = {{ E }} − Cost − Penalty (if any), where:

  • Cost = (your number)² ÷ {{ K }}. Higher numbers cost more (the cost grows with the square of your number).
  • Penalty of {{ L }} applies only if your number is strictly below the group median that round.
The group median

After everyone submits their numbers, the group median (the middle value among the {{ n }} numbers chosen) is computed and shown to you.

If your number equals the median or is above it, you pay no penalty.

If your number is strictly below the median, you pay the penalty of {{ L }}.

Payment

At the end of the experiment, one round will be selected at random and you will receive your earnings from that round, plus a fixed show-up fee.
