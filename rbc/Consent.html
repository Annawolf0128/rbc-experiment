Informed Consent

Purpose

You are invited to participate in a research study on how people make decisions in groups. The study involves choosing numbers under different conditions and observing how a group of participants jointly reach an outcome.

What you will do

You will play a 12-round economic decision-making game with other participants. In each round you choose an integer between 0 and 100. After the game, you will answer a brief survey (about 8 questions). The total session takes approximately 15–20 minutes.

Voluntary participation

Your participation is completely voluntary. You may stop at any time by closing the browser window, with no consequences. If you withdraw before completing the study, any data already collected from you will be discarded.

Data and privacy

Your responses will be stored anonymously and used only for academic research. No personally identifying information is collected. Aggregated results may be presented in academic publications and presentations.

Payment

Upon completing the study you will receive a fixed show-up fee plus your earnings from one randomly selected round of the game.
